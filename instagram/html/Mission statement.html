Mission Statement of Instagram

Instagram’s original mission statement is to capture and share the world’s moments. By creating an app to use your mobile phone and capture these moments you deem to be important, Instagram allowed users to share these moments with each other. One of the end goals of Instagram has always been to have its users share moments with each other, and make each other aware and allow each other to be part of moments all across the globe.
